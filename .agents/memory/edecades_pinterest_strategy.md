## Posting Schedule
- **Frequency:** 1-2 fresh pins per day (start with 5-7/week as new account)
- **Best times:** 12pm, 6pm, 8pm CST (Tuesdays, Thursdays, Fridays are best)
- **Pin types:** Mix of images, infographics, and quote cards

---

## Weekly Content Calendar

### Monday — Fashion Day
- Pin: Decade fashion lookbooks or style breakdowns
- Example: "What people wore in the 1970s 🕺" → link to eDecades 1970s page

### Tuesday — Music Day
- Pin: Album covers, artist spotlights, genre timelines
- Example: "Top 10 songs that defined the 1980s 🎮" → link to eDecades

### Wednesday — History & Culture Day
- Pin: Historical events, cultural moments, iconic photos
- Example: "5 things that changed in the 1960s ☮️" → link to eDecades

### Thursday — Inventions & Tech Day
- Pin: Tech timelines, invention spotlights, science moments
- Example: "Inventions from the 1990s that shaped the internet 📼"

### Friday — Sports MVPs Day
- Pin: Athlete spotlights, championship moments, hall of fame facts
- Example: "Greatest NBA MVPs of all time 🏆" → link to SportsMVPs page

### Saturday — Nostalgia/Throwback Day
- Pin: Childhood memories, retro ads, vintage photos
- Example: "Saturday morning cartoons from the 1980s 📺"

### Sunday — Quiz/Engagement Day
- Pin: "What's Your Soul Decade?" quiz card → link to eDecades Quiz page
- Pin: Trivia questions from the decade with answer reveal

## Pin Design Guidelines
- **Size:** 1000 x 1500px (2:3 ratio) — Pinterest optimal
- **Colors:** Charcoal (#333d4d) background, Rose Gold (#d4956e) accents
- **Font:** Bold, readable headline at the top
- **Logo:** eDecades watermark bottom-right corner
- **CTA:** Always include "Explore at eDecades.com" at the bottom

## Growth Tips
- Use 3-5 keywords in every pin description (no hashtag stuffing)
- Link EVERY pin back to a relevant eDecades page
- Repin top-performing content to multiple boards
- Apply for Rich Pins at: https://developers.pinterest.com/tools/url-debugger/
- Engage with nostalgia/history accounts — comment and save their pins
- Run a Pinterest ad campaign targeting ages 25-55 interested in nostalgia, history, music
